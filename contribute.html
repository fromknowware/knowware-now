04 · QUESTIONS

Three that come up most.

Why is Volume I capped at 81 voices?
Eighty-one is 9×9 — nine chapters, nine voices apiece. It is enough to be a genuine investigation and small enough to be a genuine book. Volume II, if it happens, will be its own 81.
What does "named in the acknowledgments" actually mean?
If you volunteer as a manuscript editor (Path D) and return line-level notes on your chapter, your name appears in the Volume I colophon. No tiers, no ranks — one list, one typeface, one size.
Is any of this behind a paywall?
No. Volume I will be sold in print when it ships, but every form of contribution listed here is free. We ask for your time, not your money — and only the time you offer.
